FOSSEE Image Processing Toolbox

  • align — Aligns images for HDR image creation.
  • applyTransformer — Apply affine or TPS transformation to image.
  • detectBRIEFDescriptors — Method for computing BRIEF descriptors.
  • detectDAISYDescriptors — Method for computing DAISY descriptors.
  • detectLATCHDescriptors — Method for for computing the LATCH descriptors.
  • detectSIFTFeatures — Method to find scale-invariant features.
  • detectSTARKeypoints — Method to find star-Keypoints of an image.
  • distanceExtractor — Computes shape-distance between two images.
  • histogramCostExtractor — Finds the cost matrix.
  • makeHDR — Create HDR image.
